The Inland Northwest Nature Connection unites community and nature through education and recreation programs that cultivate an informed and engaged community. Our efforts were made possible by an anonymous donor who believed in our mission to connect the people of the Inland Northwest with the natural resources that surround them. We value experiences, curiosity, and education. These things make us who we are.
